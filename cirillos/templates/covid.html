Cirillo's COVID-19 Procedures

Your safety is important to us

Guiding Principles

Cirillo's cares about the health and safety of our customers above all else. It is of upmost importance of Bob Mersereau, our owner, and our staff that we are able to give you great service while following the CDC's guidelines. This means that staff will be tested regularly, and surfaces will be cleaned often to prevent the spread of germs.

Masks

The CDC recommends the use of masks to recude the risk of COVID-19 spread. Masks will be worn by all employees and are required to be granted entry into the restaurant.

The only exceptions to this rule are:

  • Babies and children younger than 2 years old
  • Anyone who is incapacitated or otherwise unable to remove the mask without assistance
  • Anyone who is having a medical emergency

In addition to masks being worn when not eating, all tables will be at least 6 feet apart to separate diners to a safe distance from one another. Being socially distant is an important part of curbing the spread of the virus.

Shared Objects

At our tables, we will no longer be providing physical members for our patrons to reduce the risk of spread. Instead, a QR code will be at each table which you can scan with your smartphone's camera. It will open up our menu directly on your phone. Additionally, Cirillo's will be limiting the sharing of condiments and salt and pepper. Single use packets will be available at request.
